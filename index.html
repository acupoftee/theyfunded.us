The current US government has plans to dismantle the National Endowment of the Arts (NEA). Yet the NEA's modest budget amounts to less than 0.025% of the public funds spent on the US military. In the US Census in 2000, more people identified their primary occupation as artist, than as lawyer, doctor, or police officer combined. The NEA supports all art practices (like ALL of these). To support the NEA and save public funding for the arts in the USA, call your local representative.
